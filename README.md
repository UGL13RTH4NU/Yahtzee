# Yahtzee Scorecard
[🎮 Open the Yahtzee Scorecard Here](https://ugl13rth4nu.github.io/Yahtzee/)

An online, mobile-friendly HTML score sheet for playing Yahtzee.

## The Origin Story
My mom and wife play Yahtzee together all the time, usually over the phone. I built this app because I got tired of wasting so much ink and paper on printed score sheets! It is designed to seamlessly accommodate their specific style of play and automatically handle all the final math.

## Gameplay: Standard or "3-at-Once"
This scorecard supports standard gameplay, but it is specifically built to support a strategic variation of Yahtzee: **playing 3 games simultaneously**. 

* **To play 3 games at once:** Players roll and can choose to place their score in Game 1, Game 2, or Game 3. This allows players to spread out their rolls in a much more favorable manner, creating better opportunities to score high and avoid taking 0s in difficult categories.
* **To play a standard game:** You do not have to play 3 games! If you just want to play a normal, single game of Yahtzee, simply fill out the "Game 1" column and leave the rest blank. The app will calculate everything normally.

## How to Use the Scorecard (Two Ways to Play)
The app supports 2 to 8 players and features two distinct ways to keep score, depending on your preference.

### Method 1: The Quick Compare (Bring Your Own Scorecard)
*Best for remote play where everyone keeps their own detailed scores on their own devices.*
1. Select the number of players using the dropdown at the top.
2. Keep track of your own game(s) on the main screen. 
3. At the end of the game, scroll down to the **Compare Final Scores** section on your page.
4. Type in the final, combined scores of your opponents. 
5. Hit **Compare Final Scores** to automatically rank the players and announce the winner!

### Method 2: The Master Scorekeeper (Full App Tracking)
*Best if one person wants to use the app to track every single roll for every player.*
1. Select the number of players and type their names into the boxes at the top of the screen. Press **Enter** to lock a name in.
2. The name boxes act as **player tabs**. Tap any player's name to instantly switch the app to their personal, color-coded score sheet.
3. Enter their scores exactly where they want them placed.
4. The app will automatically calculate their Subtotals, Bonuses, and Grand Totals.
5. **Auto-Sync:** You don't have to manually enter anything at the end! The app automatically syncs every player's Grand Total to the main page. Just switch back to the first tab (Player 1) and hit **Compare Final Scores** to see the final rankings.

## Features
* **Auto-Saving:** The app uses local browser memory. If you accidentally close the tab, your phone goes to sleep, or you refresh the page, your game will be saved exactly where you left it.
* **Dynamic Colors:** Every player tab generates a unique color palette so you always know whose sheet you are editing.
* **Tie-Breaker Logic:** Automatically handles and displays standard ranking ties.

## Limitations
* **Fully Self-Contained (No Cloud Sync):** This app runs entirely on your specific device and does not connect to the internet to sync with other phones. If multiple people are tracking their games on their own individual devices, they will not see each other's live updates and will need to share their final totals at the end (as described in Method 1).
